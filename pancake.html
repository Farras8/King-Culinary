How To Make Pancake

I found this pancake recipe in my Grandma's recipe book. Judging from the weathered look of this recipe card, this was a family favorite.
Luna
|
May 12, 2021

Ingredient

1
1 ½ cups all-purpose flour
2
3 ½ teaspoons baking powder
3
1 tablespoon white sugar
4
¼ teaspoon salt, or more to taste
5
1 ¼ cups milk
6
3 tablespoons butter, melted

Cooking Step

1
Sift flour, baking powder, sugar, and salt together in a large bowl. Make a well in the center and add milk, melted butter, and egg; mix until smooth.
2
Heat a lightly oiled griddle or pan over medium-high heat. Pour or scoop the batter onto the griddle, using approximately 1/4 cup for each pancake.
3
cook until bubbles form and the edges are dry, about 2 to 3 minutes.
4
Flip and cook until browned on the other side. Repeat with remaining batter.
